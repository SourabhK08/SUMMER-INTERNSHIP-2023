he remainder operator % gives the remainder of the division of two numbers.

Example

5 % 2 = 1
5 / 2 = 2 remainder 1
2 * 2 = 4
5 - 4 = 1
Usage
In mathematics, a number can be checked to be even or odd by checking the 
remainder of the division of the number by 2. Even numbers have a remainder of 0, 
while odd numbers a remainder of 1.

17 % 2 = 1
48 % 2 = 0
